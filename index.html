About me

I am a postdoc at HITSZ majoring in Computer Science. I've received the doctor and master degree from Lehigh University in 2020, and bacholar degree from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2017.

My research interests include Explainable and Robust Machine Learning, Graphical Models, and Nature Language Processing.
