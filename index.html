About me

I am a 5th year Ph.D. student at Lehigh University majoring in Computer Science. I've received the master degree from Lehigh University in 2020, and bacholar degree from Beijing Jiaotong University in 2017.

My research interests include Explainable and Robust Machine Learning, and Graphical Models. I am also interested in Natural Language Processing and Graph Neural Networks.
